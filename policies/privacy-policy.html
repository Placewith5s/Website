Privacy Policy | Placewith5s

Last Updated: Mar 30, 2025

Introduction

At Placewith5s, we prioritize your privacy and are fully committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under applicable laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Information We Collect

We intentionally limit the collection of personal information. However, to maintain website functionality and optimize the user experience, we collect specific data as follows:

  1. Personal Data You Provide
  2. We do not collect personal information, such as your name or contact details, unless you voluntarily submit it through direct communication (e.g., submitting a form or contacting us). When you provide such information, it is used exclusively for the stated purposes (such as responding to inquiries).
  3. Automatically Collected Data
  4. We collect non-personally identifiable data to enhance website performance and improve the user experience. This includes:
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Date and time of visit
  • Pages visited and website interaction
  • Referring website

This data is collected through cookies and similar technologies and does not include personal identifiers.

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data as follows:

  • Maintain and improve the website
  • Analyze website performance
  • Ensure site security

We do not use your data for marketing or sell it to third parties.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that enable website functionality and enhance user experience. We use cookies to:

  • Ensure proper website functionality
  • Analyze user behavior to improve the site
  • Remember user preferences for basic functionality

We do not track personal information or use third-party advertising cookies. For more details, refer to our Cookie Policy.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services, such as analytics providers, to understand user interactions with the website. These services collect data via cookies and other technologies but operate independently with their own privacy policies. We advise reviewing their privacy practices.

Your Rights under GDPR

Under the GDPR, you are entitled to the following rights:

  • Access:
  • You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification:
  • You can request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure:
  • You can request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Restriction of Processing:
  • You can request limitations on how we process your data.
  • Data Portability:
  • You can request to transfer your data to another provider in a structured format.
  • Objection:
  • You can object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances.

To exercise your rights, contact us through our Contact Form.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to safeguard your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Encryption and other security methods are applied where applicable. However, no online transmission is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. In case of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities, following GDPR protocols.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for the duration necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or to comply with legal obligations. When the data is no longer needed, it is deleted or anonymized.

International Data Transfers

If you access our website from outside the region where our servers are located, your data may be transferred internationally. We apply GDPR-compliant safeguards for these transfers.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for individuals under 16 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We will update this Privacy Policy as needed to reflect changes in regulations or our services. The "Last Updated" date will be adjusted accordingly. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed.

Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, contact us through our Contact Form. We are committed to providing prompt and transparent responses.
