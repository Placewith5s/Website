Your Privacy Matters

Placewith5s respects your privacy and ensures that no personal data is collected through cookies or tracking technologies unless voluntarily provided. This Cookie Policy outlines our use of cookies to provide a functional and optimized website experience while protecting your privacy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They enable the website to remember your preferences, enhance your experience, and collect statistical data about site usage. Cookies can either be session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (remain on your device for a specified duration).

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Enable the basic functionality of the website
  • Provide a secure and smooth user experience
  • Analyze user behavior and website performance
  • Store user preferences for a personalized experience

Cookies are used strictly to improve website performance and functionality. We do not track personal information or use cookies for advertising purposes.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  1. Strictly Necessary Cookies
  2. These cookies are essential for the proper functioning of the website. Without them, features such as page navigation and access to secure areas would not work.
  3. Performance Cookies
  4. Performance cookies collect anonymous data that helps us measure and improve the website's performance, including page load times and user interaction metrics.
  5. Functionality Cookies
  6. Functionality cookies allow the website to remember user preferences and enhance the user experience, such as language settings or user-specific features.

We do not use third-party cookies for advertising or tracking purposes.
