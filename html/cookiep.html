About Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They help websites remember preferences, enhance user experience, and gather anonymous statistical data.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies solely for the following purposes:

  • Ensuring the website operates smoothly and securely
  • Analyzing aggregated website traffic patterns (no personal data is tracked)
  • Potentially remembering basic preferences (no personally identifiable information is stored)

Types of Cookies We Use

We primarily use the following types of cookies:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for website function.
  • Performance Cookies: Measure and improve site performance (anonymous).
  • Functionality Cookies: Enhance website functionality and personalization.

We do not use any third-party cookies for advertising or tracking.
