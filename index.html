Fine-Tune Power Management with ParkControl

Bitsum's Website For ParkControl

**Laptops:** Disable both "On Plugged In (AC)" and "On Battery (DC)" when at 100% battery. Turn off all park settings for optimal performance.

Tip: Enable "Show power plan change notifications" and "Show ParkControl settings in Windows Power Options" for easy access and monitoring.

Advanced Optimization (BACKUP FIRST!)

Backup Your Registry

  1. Click the Windows logo and type "regedit" (Registry Editor).
  2. Go to File > Export to create a backup.

Show Hidden Power Plan Settings & Fix ParkControl

  1. Open the Registry Editor.
  2. Navigate to:
    Computer\HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Control\Power\PowerSettings\54533251-82be-4824-96c1-47b60b740d00\be337238-0d82-4146-a960-4f3749d470c7
  3. Set Attributes to 2.

Optimize with Registry Editor (regedit)

  1. Navigate to:
    HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Control\PriorityControl
  2. Click Win32PrioritySeperation and choose Decimal or Hexadecimal.
  3. Enter one of the following values:
    • Gaming: Hex: 14, 15, 16, 18 | Dec: 20, 21, 22, 24
    • Other: Hex: 24, 25, 26, 2a, or 2 (default) | Dec: 36, 37, 38, 42, or 2
