About Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They assist in remembering preferences, improving user experience, and providing anonymized tracking data.

How We Use Cookies

We utilize cookies for various purposes, including:

Types of Cookies

Different types of cookies serve various functions:
