About Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They assist the website in remembering your preferences, improving your user experience, and offering website owners anonymized tracking data.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for various purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Ensuring the proper functioning of our website
  • Analyzing website traffic and user behavior
  • Personalizing your experience based on your preferences

Types of Cookies We Use

There are different types of cookies with various functions:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the basic functionality of the website.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze how users interact with our website.
  • Functionality Cookies: Allow us to remember your preferences for an improved user experience.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Placed by third-party services for analytics and advertising purposes.
